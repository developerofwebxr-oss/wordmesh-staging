# WordMesh — Brief

WordMesh is a live, walkable 3D map of the Superhero.com / aeternity word-token economy, delivered in WebXR as one link that works across desktop, mobile, Quest, and AR.

Word-tokens appear as nodes sized and brightened by their Trendminer price and volume. `.chain` users are embodied, mobile pill-shaped avatars with cut-sphere faces and floating callsigns who can walk, run, and fly through the mesh. Real buys thicken and brighten word-nodes; tips draw person-to-person support lines — the two verbs are kept distinct and never conflated. Real on-chain events travel as light-pulses down the lines connecting nodes, and the Erlang-style mesh visibly self-heals when a node drops.

Revenue is a monthly sponsorship retainer, with Aeternity/Superhero as sponsor #1, layered with an in-world economy: cosmetics, beacons, and orbit slots pay 100% to the developer, avatar gifts split 95/5 to recipient/developer, plus semantic ad inventory (affiliate links, programmatic via Zesty, direct placements, and brand-owned words). Free to roam is absolute; payment is only required to interact economically.

Delivery follows Phases 0–4 as specified in CLAUDE.md: mock mesh demo, real data, identity & presence, non-custodial writes via Superhero Wallet handoff, and the in-world economy.

Repo: github.com/developerofwebxr-oss/wordmesh. GitHub Pages is enabled, deploying from the `main` branch root.
